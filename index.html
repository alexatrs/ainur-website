How we work together

  • We start with 1‑2 sessions to understand your request and current situation.
  • I ask gentle, honest questions and reflect what I see and feel in our contact.
  • We focus on what is happening with you here and now, not only on your past.
  • You decide the pace. My role is to support, clarify, and be on your side.

This can be helpful if

  • You feel anxious, tense, or tired all the time.
  • You struggle with boundaries, guilt, or pleasing others.
  • Relationships repeat the same painful pattern.
  • You are going through a breakup, move, or other big change.
